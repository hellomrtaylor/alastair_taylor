Bowmore

Live at www.bowmore.com

Alastair spearheaded this project to redesign the website for MBD’s flagship global whisky brand, Bowmore. Working with Glasgow agency Yomego, Alastair transformed the site with a responsive layout to cater for the sites growing mobile visitor numbers. The site has allowed Bowmore to increase it’s digital marketing activities and has seen a 181% rise in visitor numbers since the redesign. The site is also a finalist in the Best Website category in the 2013 Rev Awards.
